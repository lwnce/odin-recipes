Turkey Provolone Bacon Sandwich

Really as simple as it sounds. This is one of my go to's for anything, as it's a hot and melty sandwich in less than five minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 Tablespoon Butter
  • 2 Slices White Bread
  • 2-3 Slices of your Turkey of choice
  • 1 Slice Provolone Cheese
  • Bacon Bits

Instructions

\
  1. Cut the butter into four even pieces. They will be used at different times through the process.
  2. Heat pan to medium-high heat, and place one piece of butter to melt.
  3. Take a slice of bread and soak the melted butter onto one side of the bread. Repeat with another butter and bread, then place bread to the side.
  4. Take your third butter slice and place onto the pan with your turkey. Heat and flip the turkey to your liking.
  5. When satisfied, place the provolone sheese onto the turkey and allow to melt.
  6. While this is happening, take your last slice of butter and start melting it into thr pan. Add your desired amount of bacon bits into the pan, and allow to heat and soak the butter in.
  7. Take the bacon and scoop onto the melted turkey component. Then add one slice of bread, with the buttery side face up.
  8. Flip the bread onto the pan, and place your second slice of bread onto the sandwich. Repeat the toasting on the other side.
  9. Place on to plate and cut how you desire. Enjoy!
