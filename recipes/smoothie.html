Strawberry Banana Smoothie

A childhood favorite of mine, and still amke it to this day.

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups Frozen Strawberry
  • 1 Large Banana
  • 8-10 oz. Milk
  • 1 Cup Ice
  • 1 Tablespoon Turbinado Cane Sugar
  • Optional: 1 Scoop of your favorite unflavored protein powder

Steps

  1. Add ingredients to a large blender
  2. Blend ingredients for a liberal amount fo time, adding a splash of milk to reduce thickness, if necessary.
  3. Pour and Serve. Top with fruit or cream of choice. Enjoy!
